Abdelrahman Maher

Abdelrahman Maher is a former STEM Ismailia student who earned a full scholarship to the French University in Egypt, where he is now studying Mechanical Engineering. His years at STEM strengthened his discipline, work ethic, and problem-solving abilities through challenging coursework and the pressure to maintain strong academic performance. He was honored by the French Embassy for receiving his scholarship at UFE, marking a meaningful achievement in his academic journey. He continues to build on the foundation he gained at STEM and aims to advance in engineering, research, and innovation.
