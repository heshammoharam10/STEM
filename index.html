What is STEM?

STEM is a learning approach that combines science, technology, engineering, and math. It encourages students to think critically, work together, and solve real problems.

Why is STEM Important?

  • It helps students become creative thinkers.
  • It prepares students for future jobs in science and technology.
  • It teaches teamwork and problem-solving.

Examples of STEM Fields

STEM includes many exciting areas such as:

  1. Computer Science
  2. Robotics
  3. Engineering
  4. Medicine
  5. Environmental Science
