STEM Obour is one of Egypt’s leading schools that focuses on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. Students here learn by experimenting, discovering, and creating real-world solutions.

Our students work together in teams to design creative projects that make a difference in their communities.

What Makes STEM Obour Special?

  • Modern laboratories for physics, chemistry, and biology.
  • Advanced technology and engineering workshops.
  • Friendly teachers who help students think creatively.
  • National and international competitions for innovation.
  • Projects that aim to improve life in Egypt.

Here are some amazing things STEM Obour students have achieved:

  1. Building robots to help hospital staff and clean the environment.
  2. Creating solar-powered systems to produce clean energy.
  3. Designing smart cities with safe and efficient transport.
  4. Developing new ideas to recycle waste and protect nature.

At STEM Obour, every student learns to explore, invent, and dream big — building the future of Egypt, one project at a time!
